About LOOΠΔ

LOONA is a South Korean girl group created by BlockBerryCreative. The group includes 12 members and 3 sub-units. From October 2016 to June 2018, each member was revealed with a solo single album. The whole group debuted on August 19, 2018.

  • LOOΠΔ 1/3
  • Odd Eye Circle
  • yyxy

LOONA 1/3 is the first sub-unit of LOONA. The members include HeeJin, HyunJin, HaSeul, and ViVi. They debuted on March 12, 2017 with their song "Love & Live".

ODD EYE CIRCLE is the second sub-unit of LOONA. The members include Kim Lip, JinSoul, and Choerry. They debuted on September 21, 2017 with their mini-album Mix & Match.

yyxy is the third sub-unit of LOONA. The members include Yves, Chuu, Go Won, and Olivia Hye. They debuted on May 30, 2018 with their mini-album Beauty & The Beat.
